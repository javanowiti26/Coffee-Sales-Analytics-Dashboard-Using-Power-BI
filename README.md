# ☕ Coffee Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

The Coffee Sales Performance Dashboard is a Business Intelligence project developed using Power BI to analyze coffee sales data and generate actionable insights for business decision-making. The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer behavior, product demand, pricing effectiveness, and geographic trends.

By transforming raw transactional data into interactive visualizations, the project enables stakeholders to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify growth opportunities, and optimize business strategies.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance and revenue trends.
* Identify top-performing coffee products and roast types.
* Evaluate customer purchasing behavior and retention.
* Measure the impact of loyalty programs on sales.
* Assess customer lifetime value (CLV).
* Analyze revenue contribution by product category and price range.
* Identify the most profitable pricing segments.
* Support data-driven decision-making through interactive dashboards.

## Key Business Insights

* Medium Roast products generated the highest sales volume and revenue.
* Repeat customers contributed significantly to total revenue.
* Loyalty card holders demonstrated higher purchase frequency and spending patterns.
* Mid-priced products delivered the strongest balance between sales volume and profitability.
* A small percentage of customers generated a substantial share of total revenue.
* Geographic analysis identified high-performing markets and potential growth opportunities.
